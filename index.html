Intro

Three words for Louis: strong-willed, fast-learner, effective.

Louis Owen is a Data Science enthusiast who always hungry for new knowledge. He pursued a Mathematics major at one of the top university in Indonesia, Institut Teknologi Bandung, under the full final-year scholarship.

Louis is an NLP Enthusiast. However, he is also familiar working with tabular data and Computer Vision as well. Louis has experienced as an analytics / machine learning intern in various field of industry, including OTA (Traveloka), e-Commerce (Tokopedia), FinTech (Do-it), Smart City App (Qlue Smart City) and currently as a Data Science Consultant at The World Bank. Working in the same team with Louis is awesome and memorable, as stated by his colleagues. During the spare time, Louis spent his time to enjoy his hobbies: watching movies, conduct side-projects, writing articles

He also loves to give back to the community by sharing his experiences and knowledge.
